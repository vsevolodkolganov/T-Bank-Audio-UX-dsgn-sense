# Audio Brand Identity
T-Bank Audio UX

## Concept

When the visual interface disappears, the **sound becomes the brand**.

Audio identity ensures the user recognizes the service instantly.

---

# Brand Personality

The voice and sound should feel:

- confident
- warm
- intelligent
- calm
- human

Avoid:

- robotic tone
- banking bureaucracy
- overly playful sounds

---

# Core Brand Sound

Signature interval:

Major fifth.

Notes example:

C – G

Meaning:

- stability
- trust
- openness

Used in:

success signals  
system start  
brand logo

---

# Sonic Logo

Structure:

1. Soft inhale tone
2. Major fifth chord
3. Short resonance tail

Duration:

800 ms

Purpose:

Brand recognition.

---

# Voice Design

Voice characteristics:

gender-neutral  
warm mid-range  
clear articulation  
low tempo

Recommended speaking speed:

160 words per minute.

---

# Dialogue Style

Instead of formal banking speech:

❌ "Операция успешно выполнена."

Use:

✅ "Готово."

Short phrases reduce cognitive load.

---

# Earcon Language

Earcons form a **functional sound vocabulary**.

Examples:

listen → rising tone  
processing → low pulse  
success → major chord  
error → descending dissonance

---

# Sound Texture System

Financial information can be represented as sound density.

Example:

balance query in privacy mode

low density texture → low balance  
medium density texture → medium balance  
dense harmonic texture → high balance

---

# Spatial Audio

Audio direction provides extra meaning.

Outgoing transfer:
sound pans right.

Incoming transfer:
sound pans left.

---

# Emotional Goal

The interface should feel like:

a calm conversation with a trusted assistant.
