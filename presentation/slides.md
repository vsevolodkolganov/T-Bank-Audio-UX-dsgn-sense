# Slide 1
Title

T-Bank Audio Interface

# Slide 2
The Problem

Modern banking apps require constant visual attention.

But many everyday situations make screen interaction difficult:

driving  
walking  
public transport  
carrying bags

---

# Slide 3
Key Insight

People already interact with technology through sound.

Voice assistants  
headphones  
smart speakers

Audio can become a new financial interface.

---

# Slide 4
The Idea

T-Bot — voice-first banking.

Users can:

check balance  
transfer money  
pay for purchases

without opening the app.

---

# Slide 5
Audio Interface Language

Instead of visual elements:

buttons → earcons  
loading → sound pulse  
notifications → tonal signals

Sound becomes the interface.

---

# Slide 6
Smart Silence

The assistant adapts to context:

Silent mode  
Micro mode  
Conversational mode  
Ambient mode

The system never talks more than necessary.

---

# Slide 7
Security

Voice biometrics  
anti-spoofing  
confirmation for large transfers

Financial actions remain safe.

---

# Slide 8
Example Interaction

User:

"Переведи 350 рублей Маше"

System:

🎧 listening tone  
🎧 confirmation  
🎧 success chord

Voice:
"Готово."

Total time ≈ 2 seconds.

# Slide 9
Benefits

Faster micro-transactions
